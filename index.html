A Colossal Text Adventure

Welcome to Colossal Text Adventure, a game engine demo to make and distribute classic feeling text adventure games to modern audiences.

You walk into the Lost Woods in search of the Forest Temple, fabled to be hidden for centuries deep within the heart of these cursed woods. The Lost Woods are known to take people in, but never give them back.
Don't be surprised if you get turned around navigating this maze of a forest. Use your map, and try to keep your directions straight!
